Status: **PARKED — future work, not currently being built.**

Current focus: Interactive district map (see `SPEC-DISTRICT-MAP.md`). Citizen reporting is the *next* major feature after the map and information layer are solid. Resume this spec only after the map ships.

---

## The line we're staying inside

> We are **not** a speech platform. We are a **structured civic reporting platform** with social features.

- Structured submissions only (form-driven, category-driven, location-tied)
- Social features (likes, comments, shares) attach to **issues**, not to profiles or opinions
- No free-form posting, no follower graph, no timeline of thoughts
- Neutrality positioning is preserved — we host verified civic issues, not political speech

---

## Question 1 — Scope of issues (DECIDED)

**Decision: Tier 1 — Infrastructure only for v1.**

Accepted categories:
- Potholes / broken roads
- Streetlights (non-functional / missing)
- Garbage / solid waste
- Water supply issues
- Drainage / sewage overflow
- Public toilets (broken / missing)

Explicitly **out of scope for v1**:
- Scheme-delivery grievances (Tier 2) — planned for v2, needs different submission flow
- Governance / political grievances (Tier 3) — not planned, too high-risk for a single-operator platform

**Why:** Lowest moderation burden, highest visual verifiability, zero direct political charge. Gets the submission flow, moderation pipeline, and officials-routing infra battle-tested before we touch anything politically sensitive.
